Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you work with businesses that are behind on their books? ▾
Yes. Cleanup and catch-up services are available before monthly service begins. We'll assess where things stand and develop a plan to bring your books current.
Do you replace a CPA or tax preparer? ▾
No. We work alongside your CPA and provide clean, accurate books for tax filing — reducing their time and your cost at year-end.
Can I get payroll services without bookkeeping? ▾
Payroll services are available as part of a bookkeeping engagement, which ensures payroll transactions are properly recorded and reconciled within your financial records.
Do you work with businesses outside the DC metro area? ▾
Yes, though our primary focus is the Washington DC metro area. We work remotely with clients and leverage QuickBooks Online to keep everything accessible.
How are your packages priced? ▾
Flat-rate monthly fees based on your transaction volume and business complexity. We provide a custom quote after a discovery call so you know exactly what to expect.
